ISMIR 2009 Corporate Support

The 10th International Society for Music Information Retrieval Conference would like to offer sponsorship opportunities and exhibit space for interested organizations. Your support will be clearly visible to the key personnel in the field of Music Information Retrieval and related fields. The sponsorship is available at three different levels as indicated below.
Please contact Ichiro Fujinaga (ich [at] music.mcgill.ca) in English or Masataka Goto (m.goto [at] aist.go.jp) in Japanese for further information.

Gold: 500,000 JPY or 5,000 USD

  • Exhibition space during demonstration session
  • Large and prominent placement of logo on conference banners
  • Half-page logo in conference program
  • Large, separated logo in printed proceedings
  • Two complimentary conference registrations

Silver: 300,000 JPY or 3,000 USD

  • Exhibition space during demonstration session
  • Medium logo on conference banners
  • Quarter-page logo in conference program
  • Medium, separated logo in printed proceedings
  • One complimentary conference registration

Bronze: 100,000 JPY or 1,000 USD

  • Exhibition space during demonstration session
  • Small logo on conference banners
  • Eighth-page logo in conference program
  • Small logo in printed proceedings
