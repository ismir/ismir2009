Cancellation Policy

In case of cancellation, your registration fee will be refunded after deducting the cancellation fee of 6000 JPY before 23:59 (GMT+9) of August 31, 2009. Nothing will be refunded after September 1, 2009.

Registration Desk

The registration desk is located on the 3rd floor of the Kobe International Conference Center. Registration is possible on every day of the conference, starting 9:15 am on Monday, 8:15 am on Tuesday, and 8:35 am on Wednesday to Friday. The registration desk will be open until 6:00 pm on Monday to Thursday and until 11:00 am on Friday.
